# Samyak Vascular Education (RCVIR)

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for **Samyak Vascular Education**. We are the developers behind [RCVIR.org](https://rcvir.org), a premier medical education and Website-as-a-Service (WaaS) platform designed for modern diagnostic centers and interventional radiologists.

## Our Technology Stack
We bridge the gap between clinical radiology, interventional radiology and advanced medical informatics. Our current infrastructure leverages:

* **Interactive Imaging:** Advanced integration of the OHIF DICOM viewer for web-based, scrollable clinical cases.
* **Artificial Intelligence:** Utilization of state-of-the-art vision models, including SAM 3 and MONAI, for medical image segmentation and analysis.
* **Automated Data Pipelines:** Robust backend workflows utilizing n8n, LlamaParse, and Qdrant vector databases to process, retrieve, and generate high-yield medical literature (RAG architecture).
* **Enterprise Infrastructure:** Scalable Website-as-a-Service (WaaS) deployment with secure domain mapping for localized diagnostic centers.
